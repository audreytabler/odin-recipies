Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic dish of layed pasta, meat and cheese. This recipe is sure to delight friends and family alike!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese mixture
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 360 degrees
  2. Cook ground beef and sausage
  3. Layer cheese, sauce and meat between noodles.
  4. Bake for 40 minutes
